Research Areas

Advancing the Future of Healthcare Through Technology

Advanced Fetal and Infant Neuroimaging Analytics

Developing state-of-the-art deep learning architectures for enhanced neuroimaging analysis, including automated volumetric segmentation, motion-robust reconstruction, and synthetic data generation. Our innovative approaches integrate multimodal neuroimaging data to decode complex neurodevelopmental trajectories and elucidate early brain development patterns.

Early Neurodevelopmental Risk Pathway Analysis

Investigating complex mechanistic pathways and gene-environment interactions that influence early neurodevelopmental trajectories, utilizing advanced computational methods to elucidate biological mechanisms underlying developmental vulnerability and resilience for early detection and intervention strategies.

AI-Driven Mental Health Informatics

Developing advanced machine learning frameworks and digital biomarkers for mental health assessment and intervention, leveraging multimodal data integration and personalized AI algorithms to enhance early detection and therapeutic outcomes in neurodevelopmental and behavioral health domains.
